Convened 2026-07-03. Convener/Clerk: the repo's tending agent (Fable), running the practice cold from SKILL.md as its own dogfood. Three lights, dispatched in parallel (Haiku tier), each a file speaking in first person per the default rule ("prefer files over abstract concerns"): SKILL.md; docs/quaker-correspondence.md; the pair roles/clerk.md + roles/recording-clerk.md.

## Verdict / sense of the meeting

The line recently drawn in roles/clerk.md ("How to collect the ministry," ~line 43) — **Clerk does real-time, provisional ground-sensing; Recording Clerk does the formal, permanent fact-check that lands in the minute** — is real and correct. SKILL.md (step 4 and Role summary) and docs/quaker-correspondence.md (Clerk section ~line 23; mapping table) **do not contradict it; they compress both functions into the Clerk** because they were written before the split.

The shared reason all three lights touched independently: the distinction matters because **the power concentration lives in what the permanent record carries forward** — the Recording Clerk's formal check is signed into the minute; the Clerk's sensing is ephemeral. A summary that says "the Clerk fact-checks" is not false, but it is imprecise about exactly the point the repo's honesty depends on (and that a Quaker reader will scrutinize).

Disposition: no correction is *mandated* — the premise "these docs are stale" was verified by all three lights as **partly correct but misframed: compressed, not contradictory**. However, the two compressed files, ministering for themselves, each chose to sharpen their own wording. Their standing to self-revise is honored; the edits proceed as the files' own revisions, not as the convener overruling H1.
